About SANKALP

SANKALP is the flagship technical symposium of the Department of Mechanical Engineering. It serves as a crucible where theoretical knowledge meets practical application, fostering an ecosystem of curiosity and innovation. We invite you to witness a convergence of intellect and imagination where we don't just observe technology; we define it.
